Aloo Chaat

Description

Aloo Chaat is a very famous street food in India. It's a delicious and tangy dish that can be made in a short time.

For the recipe, you need boiled potatoes, some chaat masala, tamarind chutney, and green chutney. Mix all the ingredients, and you're good to go!

Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chaat masala powder
  • Coriander chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sweetened yogurt
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Lemon juice
  • Nylon sev and Coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Make Coriander Chutney (Green Chutney) using Coriander Leaves, Mint Leaves, Green Chillies
  3. Prepare Tamaring Chutney using Tamarind Pulp, Jaggery/Sugar, and some ground spices. You can also serve this Chutney with Samosas, Aloo Tikkis, etc. Also, it lasts really long!
  4. Assemble all the ingredients together
  5. Mix mashed potatoes with chilled Yogurt and Chutneys.
  6. Add Chaat masala and chopped Onions and Tomatoes
  7. Add Nylon sev and Coriander Leaves on top to for best taste. Serve it moderately hot
