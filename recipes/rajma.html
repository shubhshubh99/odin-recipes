Rajma

Description

Rajma is a popular vegetarian dish from the Indian subcontinent, consisting of red kidney beans in an onion-tomato-based thick gravy with many aromatic Indian spices. It is usually served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Kidney beans
  • Oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Bay leaf
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Red chilli powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Kasuri methi
  • Cilantro
  • Amchur powder/Lime juice

Steps

  1. Soak rajma for at least 4+ hours in hot water or overnight. Then, drain the water and keep it aside.
  2. Press SAUTE mode on Instant Pot. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and let the cumin splutter.
  3. Next, add onions, ginger-garlic paste, and saute until onions turn light brown for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Next, add finely chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree) and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Then add the spices red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, kidney beans, salt, and water. Stir well.
  6. Close the lid on the pot, and turn the pressure valve to the SEALING position. Set the pot to "BEAN/CHILI" Mode and set the timer to 30 minutes.
  7. Once the pot beeps, Do a NATURAL PRESSURE RELEASE (NPR).
  8. Lastly, remove the lid away from you, add garam masala powder, kasuri methi, cilantro, and amchur powder or lime juice. Mix well. Don't worry if the curry is too liquid consistency.
  9. Turn on SAUTE mode again and boil the curry for 5 minutes or until you get the desired consistency. If you want thicker and creamy gravy, you can mash a ladle full of rajma and add them back to the curry.
  10. Instant Pot Rajma Masala/Red Kidney Bean Curry recipe is ready, serve hot with pooris, chapatis, roti & parathas.
